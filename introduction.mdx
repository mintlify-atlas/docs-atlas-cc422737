NocoDB is the fastest and easiest way to build databases online. It transforms your databases into smart spreadsheets with a rich interface, enabling you to build powerful applications without writing code.

## What is NocoDB?

NocoDB provides a no-code interface for databases that makes them as easy to use as spreadsheets. Most internet businesses use either spreadsheets or databases to solve their needs. While spreadsheets are used by billions of people daily, databases are far more powerful for computing but harder to work with. NocoDB bridges this gap.

## Why NocoDB?

Most internet businesses equip themselves with either spreadsheet or a database to solve their business needs. Spreadsheets are used by Billion+ humans collaboratively every single day. However, we are way off working at similar speeds on databases which are way more powerful tools when it comes to computing.

Attempts to solve this with SaaS offerings have meant:
- Horrible access controls
- Vendor lock-in
- Data lock-in
- Abrupt price changes
- A glass ceiling on what's possible in the future

NocoDB solves these problems by providing an open-source, self-hosted solution that gives you complete control over your data and infrastructure.
